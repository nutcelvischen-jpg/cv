Assistant Professor, Department of International Business

I am a researcher and Assistant Professor at National Taichung University of Science and Technology. I earned my PhD from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University in 2020 and served as a visiting scholar at North Carolina State University.

I am passionate about extracting insights from data to solve complex problems. My recent work on simulating the EU's circular economy using deep learning was funded by the National Science and Technology Council.

Outside of work, I bring humor and a passion for sports to every team I join.
